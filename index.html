About Us

Located in the foothills of the Trikuta Mountains, The Royal Stay is a boutique hotel offering smartly furnished rooms in addition to a host of facilities. The Royal Stay provides luxurious and modern amenities along with a rooftop restaurant where guests can rejuvenate after a long trek to Vaishno Devi. All this, at an unbeatable value.
